1. Wash all the vegetables until clean

2. Cut the vegetables according to taste

3. Put all the chopped vegetables into a separate bowl ( small bowl ) then set aside

4. Prepare all the sauce ingredients, and then mix all of them into a small bowl

5. Take another bowl ( big bowl ) then put all the chopped vegetables in the bowl

6. Pour the dressing sauce into the bowl filled with vegetable mix 

Enjoy your Thousand island salad~
