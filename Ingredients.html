Ingredients needed : 

  1. Cherry tomatoes
  2. Red beans 
  3. Sweet corns 
  4. Lettuce
  5. Cucumber
  6. Almond ( optional )
  7. Spicy mayo/mayo + hot sauce
  8. Lemon juice
  9. Pepper powder
  10. Honey
  11. Salt

Tools needed : 

  • Container/basin for washing vegetables
  • Knife to cut the vegetables of course 
  • Cutting board
  • 1 main bowl for the salad
  • Several small bowls or container for the chopped vegetables and for the sauce
  • Spoon for mixing & stirring 
  • Pot to boil red beans & corns
